# Code of Conduct

This repository is expected to remain professional, respectful, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Keep feedback specific, useful, and focused on the work.
- Assume good intent while still addressing problems directly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Cristian Montoya may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, and block participants who do not follow this code of conduct.
